Team Scenarios:
Interdependent Sports Team
Examples: Football, rugby, soccer
Non-examples: Track and field, swimming
Good team qualities:
-Communication
-Coordination
-Trust between members
-Proper leadership, i.e. good coach and team captain
-Encouragement

Battleship Crew
Good team qualities:
-All of the above
-Following chain of command
-Trust in the mission, e.g. it's accomplishable, and we aill accomplish it
